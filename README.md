### 🔬 About Me

> Bridging the gap between structured graph knowledge and multimodal sensory data reasoning.

I am a graduate student deeply invested in advancing **Knowledge Graph Completion (KGC)** and **Multimodal Knowledge Graphs**. My research aims to unravel complex data reasoning by integrating structured graph knowledge with text, images, and other sensory data modalities.

- 🔭 **Currently Focusing on:** Multimodal Knowledge Graph Completion (MKGC) datasets (e.g., MKG-W, MKG-Y), cross-modal alignment reasoning, and analogy generation.
- 🛠️ **Workflow:** VS Code, tmux, LaTeX (Overleaf), and Alibaba Cloud infrastructure.
